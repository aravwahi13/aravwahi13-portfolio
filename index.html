About Me

My experience includes serving as Sergeant at Arms for the Rotary Club for 2 years, where I managed events and coordination. I was also part of the Interact Club at the district level for about 3 years, organising drives and events which more often than not included students from around 5-10 schools each time. Additionally, I have also actively contributed to the Setu Foundation, teaching basics of computer, algebra, and science to underprivileged children.

I believe I am a perfect fit for this position due to my proven track record of leadership and teamwork, especially during my time with the Rotary Club and the Interact Club. As Sergeant at Arms, I honed my organisational and management skills, overseeing events that involved multiple schools. Additionally, my experience teaching computer basics and algebra with the Setu Foundation reflects my ability to communicate complex ideas clearly, a critical skill for this role.
